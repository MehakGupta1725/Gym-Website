Equipments Summary

Dumbbell

Benefits:

Improves muscle strength and endurance, great for overall fitness.

Treadmill

Benefits:

Helps with cardio, weight loss, and increasing stamina.

Jump Rope

Benefits:

Great for cardio, enhances agility and foot coordination.

Kettlebell

Benefits:

Full-body workout, improves balance, and builds muscle.

Resistance Bands

Benefits:

Strengthens muscles, portable, and low-impact.

Yoga Mat

Benefits:

Enhances comfort during floor exercises, great for yoga and stretching.

Exercise Bike

Benefits:

Boosts cardio endurance, low-impact on joints.

Rowing Machine

Benefits:

Full-body workout, improves cardiovascular health.

Pull-Up Bar

Benefits:

Builds upper body strength, enhances back and biceps.

Medicine Ball

Benefits:

Improves explosive power, enhances coordination.
